User Rights

As a user of Evo 33, you have explicit rights including:

  • Transparency: Clear information about how your data is used.
  • Access and Control: Review, modify, or delete your personal information at any time.
  • Right to Opt-out: You can opt-out from any form of data tracking or personalized services.
  • Data Portability: Request and receive a copy of your data in a structured format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us directly at founder@aibiosynergy.org.
